# Guest Portal User Guide

## Overview
The Guest Portal is a simple, clean interface that allows guests to manage their bookings, orders, and account information. It's designed to be minimal, mobile-friendly, and easy to use.

## Accessing the Portal

### Login
1. Navigate to `/guest/login`
2. Enter your **Booking Reference** (e.g., HTL-1A2B3C)
3. Enter your **Email** or **Phone Number** (used during booking)
4. Click "Sign In"

### What You Can Access
Once logged in, you can:
- View your dashboard with quick overview
- See upcoming and past bookings
- Track active food and drink orders
- Submit reviews
- Contact support
- View notifications
- Manage your profile

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard provides quick access to three main areas:

### 1. Upcoming Bookings
- Click the card to see all your upcoming reservations
- View check-in dates, room types, and booking status
- Order food for your stay (if checked in)

### 2. Active Orders
- See all your current food and drink orders
- Track order status (Preparing, Ready, etc.)
- View order details and estimated times

### 3. Past Bookings
- View your complete booking history
- Download receipts
- See all past stays

---

## Bookings

### Viewing Booking Details
1. Click on any booking from the dashboard or bookings pages
2. See complete information:
   - Room type and number
   - Check-in and check-out dates
   - Total cost and payment status
   - Booking reference number

### Actions Available
- **Download Receipt** - Get a copy of your booking receipt
- **Contact Support** - Reach out for help
- **Modify Booking** - Request changes (for pending/confirmed bookings)
- **Order Food** - Place room service orders (when checked in)

---

## Orders

### Viewing All Orders
- Navigate to "Orders" in the menu
- See two sections:
  - **Current Orders** - Active orders being processed
  - **Past Orders** - Completed or cancelled orders

### Order Details
Click any order to see:
- **Status Timeline** - Visual progress from Pending to Completed
- **Items List** - All items with quantities and prices
- **Total Amount** - Complete order total
- **Notes** - Special instructions or notes
- **Estimated Time** - Preparation time (when preparing)

### Actions
- **View Full Details** - See complete order information
- **Download Receipt** - Get order receipt
- **Reorder** - Quickly reorder completed orders

---

## Reviews

### Submitting a Review
1. Navigate to "Reviews" in the menu
2. Click on the star rating (1-5 stars)
3. Enter a review title (optional)
4. Write your review comment
5. Select a category (Overall, Room, Service, Food)
6. Click "Submit Review"

### Viewing Reviews
- **My Reviews** - See all reviews you've submitted
- **What Others Are Saying** - Browse approved reviews from other guests
- **Overall Rating** - See average rating and total review count

### Review Status
- **Pending** - Awaiting admin approval
- **Approved** - Visible to all guests

## Contact

### Contact Information
The contact page shows:
- Phone number (click to call)
- Email address (click to email)
- Physical address
- WhatsApp link (if available)
- Business hours

### Sending a Message
1. Select a subject from the dropdown:
   - Booking Inquiry
   - Modify Booking
   - Cancel Booking
   - Room Service
   - Complaint
   - Compliment
   - General Question
2. Enter your booking reference (optional)
3. Write your message
4. Click "Send Message"

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?
- Double-check your booking reference
- Ensure email/phone matches your booking
- Contact support if you continue having issues

### Orders Not Showing?
- Make sure you used the same email/phone when placing the order
- Orders may take a few moments to appear
- Contact support if orders are missing

### Review Not Showing?
- Reviews must be approved by admin first
- Check "My Reviews" to see pending status
- Approved reviews appear in "What Others Are Saying"
